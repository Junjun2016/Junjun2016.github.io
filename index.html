About Me (Google Scholar)

I am currently a PhD Candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, advised by Prof. Lixu Gu. I will graduate in 2021. Now, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Yu Qiao at Multimedia Lab (MMLAB) of Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Research Interests

  • Dense prediction: 2D image segmentation, 3D medical image segmentation, objection detection, instance segmentation, scene text recognition, super resolution, etc.
  • Efficient: convolution factorization, model compression, hashing, quantization, AutoML, NAS, etc.
